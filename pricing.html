Frequently Asked Questions

What is a campaign credit?

A campaign credit represents one influencer collaboration. For example, if you run a campaign with 5 influencers, it will consume 5 credits from your monthly allowance.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get prorated credit for your current plan. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

Do you offer custom plans?

Yes, we offer custom plans for agencies and brands with specific needs. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and NEFT/RTGS transfers. All prices are in INR and exclude GST.
